FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Q1. Are there any pre-requisites?

No, but we recommend that you are able to swim 50 metres comfortably within 2 minutes.

Q2. When will the registration period close?

The registration period will close on Monday, 8th February 2015 at 2359hrs. We wish to regret and inform you that late registration will not be entertained.

Q3. What should I do after I have submitted the online registration form for mass swim?

You will receive a confirmation email within the next 5 working days, which includes a set of instructions for the payment and collection of event ticket.

Q4. Where and what time do I report on Friday 26th February 2015?

At the entrance of NTU Sports & Recreation Centre Swimming Pool. Registration opens at 1600hrs and closes at 1730hrs.

Q5. What time will the event start?

1700hrs sharp unless subjected to unforeseen weather conditions.

Q6. Where can I collect the goodie bag?

The goodie bag will be given to you on the event day upon registration.

Q7. What should I bring on the event day?

You are required to bring the event ticket and your IC for registration. If you have lost the ticket, please email to us at lgcswimfest@e.ntu.edu.sg.

Q8. I am feeling unwell, but I think I am able to swim. Should I participate in Mass Swim?

Participants who are feeling unwell should approach any of the NTU Swimfest Officials for advice. You are advised not to take part if you are feeling unwell.

Q9. What are the available transport arrangements to NTU Sports & Recreation Centre Swimming Pool?

Bus 179: From Boon Lay MRT bus interchange

NTU Campus Weekend Rider: From Pioneer MRT Station bus stop

Car: You may park at Carpark K, S&RC Zone 3a, please refer to http://www.ntu.edu.sg/has/Carparks/Pages/Zones_Charges.aspx
